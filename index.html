Questions, answered

Everything you need to know before starting a project with Aravosh.

What does Aravosh do?

Aravosh is an early-stage technology studio that builds practical software, AI automation, product design, cloud delivery and technical strategy for teams that need a clearly scoped project done well.

Is Aravosh a large agency?

No. Aravosh is a small, early-stage studio, not a large agency with a long client roster. Projects get founder-led attention rather than being handed off to a big account team.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on scope: a small MVP can launch in under a month, a standard build typically runs two to three months, and larger engagements are scoped as ongoing, phased work.

How is pricing structured?

There's no fixed price list. After an initial conversation about scope, timeline and constraints, we follow up with a clear, tailored estimate rather than a generic quote.

Can Aravosh help with AI automation or RAG projects?

Yes. We build AI-assisted workflows, internal copilots and retrieval-augmented (RAG) tools that connect models to your documents, CRMs and internal processes.

Does Aravosh build multi-agent AI systems?

Yes. We design and build multi-agent systems — coordinated AI agents that plan, hand off tasks and use tools together — for workflows like research, support triage and multi-step operations automation.

What technologies do you build with?

Our core stack includes Next.js/React and TypeScript on the frontend, Node.js and Python/FastAPI on the backend, PostgreSQL, vector search and LLM integrations for AI work, and AWS with Docker, Kubernetes and Terraform for cloud and DevOps.
